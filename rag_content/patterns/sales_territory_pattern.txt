PATTERN: Sales Territory Analysis

WHEN TO USE:
- Questions about sales territories, regions
- Territory performance analysis
- "By territory", "by region", "which territory"

TABLES INVOLVED:
- FactInternetSales (fact table - sales transactions)
- DimSalesTerritory (dimension - territory information)

JOIN PATH:
FROM dbo.FactInternetSales fis
INNER JOIN dbo.DimSalesTerritory st ON fis.SalesTerritoryKey = st.SalesTerritoryKey

KEY TERRITORY COLUMNS:
- SalesTerritoryRegion (region name, e.g., "Northwest", "Southwest", "Canada")
- SalesTerritoryCountry (country, e.g., "United States", "Canada", "United Kingdom")
- SalesTerritoryGroup (group, e.g., "North America", "Europe", "Pacific")

IMPORTANT DISTINCTION:
Sales Territory vs Customer Geography:
- Sales Territory: Assigned sales region (from DimSalesTerritory)
- Customer Geography: Where customer is located (from DimGeography)
- These can be DIFFERENT - customer in California might be in "Southwest" or "Pacific" territory

EXAMPLE QUERY - Sales by Territory:
SELECT
    st.SalesTerritoryRegion,
    st.SalesTerritoryCountry,
    SUM(fis.SalesAmount) AS TotalRevenue,
    COUNT(DISTINCT fis.SalesOrderNumber) AS OrderCount,
    COUNT(DISTINCT fis.CustomerKey) AS UniqueCustomers
FROM dbo.FactInternetSales fis
INNER JOIN dbo.DimSalesTerritory st ON fis.SalesTerritoryKey = st.SalesTerritoryKey
GROUP BY st.SalesTerritoryRegion, st.SalesTerritoryCountry
ORDER BY TotalRevenue DESC

EXAMPLE QUERY - Territory Performance with Rankings:
SELECT
    st.SalesTerritoryRegion,
    SUM(fis.SalesAmount) AS TotalRevenue,
    RANK() OVER (ORDER BY SUM(fis.SalesAmount) DESC) AS RevenueRank,
    CAST(SUM(fis.SalesAmount) / NULLIF(COUNT(DISTINCT fis.SalesOrderNumber), 0) AS DECIMAL(10,2)) AS AvgOrderValue
FROM dbo.FactInternetSales fis
INNER JOIN dbo.DimSalesTerritory st ON fis.SalesTerritoryKey = st.SalesTerritoryKey
GROUP BY st.SalesTerritoryRegion
ORDER BY TotalRevenue DESC

TERRITORIES IN DATA:
- North America: Northwest, Southwest, Canada, Central
- Europe: United Kingdom, France, Germany
- Pacific: Australia

NOTES:
- Use SalesTerritoryRegion for most queries (most specific)
- SalesTerritoryGroup for high-level analysis (North America, Europe, Pacific)
- DimSalesTerritory also connects to DimEmployee (sales reps assigned to territories)
- Use INNER JOIN as every internet sale has a territory assigned
