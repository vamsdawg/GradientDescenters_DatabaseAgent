PATTERN: Internet Sales with Product Information

WHEN TO USE:
- User asks about internet sales and products together
- Questions about product performance, sales by product
- Revenue analysis by product attributes (name, color, size, etc.)

TABLES INVOLVED:
- FactInternetSales (fact table - sales transactions)
- DimProduct (dimension - product details)

JOIN PATH:
FROM dbo.FactInternetSales fis
INNER JOIN dbo.DimProduct p ON fis.ProductKey = p.ProductKey

KEY COLUMNS TO USE:
From FactInternetSales:
- SalesAmount (revenue)
- OrderQuantity (units sold)
- UnitPrice (price per unit)
- TotalProductCost (cost)
- SalesOrderNumber (order ID)

From DimProduct:
- EnglishProductName (product name for display)
- Color, Size (product attributes)
- ListPrice (standard price)
- StandardCost (cost to manufacture)

EXAMPLE QUERY:
SELECT
    p.EnglishProductName,
    SUM(fis.SalesAmount) AS TotalRevenue,
    SUM(fis.OrderQuantity) AS UnitsSold,
    COUNT(DISTINCT fis.SalesOrderNumber) AS OrderCount
FROM dbo.FactInternetSales fis
INNER JOIN dbo.DimProduct p ON fis.ProductKey = p.ProductKey
GROUP BY p.EnglishProductName, p.ProductKey
ORDER BY TotalRevenue DESC

NOTES:
- Always use INNER JOIN (not LEFT JOIN) as every sale has a product
- Include ProductKey in GROUP BY even if not in SELECT (ensures uniqueness)
- Use EnglishProductName for display (not ProductAlternateKey)
