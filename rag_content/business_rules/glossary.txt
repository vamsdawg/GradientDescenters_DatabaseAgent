BUSINESS GLOSSARY - ADVENTUREWORKSDW2025

Quick reference for business terms and concepts in the AdventureWorksDW database.

===============================================
SALES & REVENUE TERMS
===============================================

Revenue:
  Total SalesAmount from sales transactions. Includes product price, tax, and freight.

Internet Sales:
  Direct-to-consumer online orders stored in FactInternetSales table.

Reseller Sales:
  Business-to-business wholesale orders stored in FactResellerSales table.
  Resellers purchase in bulk and resell to end consumers.

Sales Amount:
  Total charged to customer for an order line including tax and freight.

Order:
  A single purchase transaction identified by unique SalesOrderNumber.
  Can contain multiple line items (products).

Order Line:
  Individual product within an order. One order can have many lines.

Sales Channel:
  Method of sale - either Internet (B2C) or Reseller (B2B).

===============================================
PRODUCT TERMS
===============================================

Product:
  Individual item for sale (e.g., "Mountain-200 Black, 42").

Product Category:
  Top-level product grouping: Bikes, Accessories, Clothing, Components.

Product Subcategory:
  Mid-level grouping under category (e.g., "Mountain Bikes", "Helmets").

Product Hierarchy:
  Three-level structure: Category → Subcategory → Product.

Finished Goods:
  Complete, saleable products (not raw materials or components in assembly).
  Identified by FinishedGoodsFlag = 1.

List Price:
  Manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP) for a product.

Standard Cost:
  Cost to manufacture or acquire the product.

Unit Price:
  Actual selling price per unit in a transaction (may differ from list price due to discounts).

SKU / Product Code:
  ProductAlternateKey - the product identifier/stock keeping unit.

===============================================
CUSTOMER TERMS
===============================================

Customer:
  Individual who has purchased or is registered in the system.
  Stored in DimCustomer.

Active Customer:
  Customer who has made at least one purchase (exists in FactInternetSales).

Customer Segment:
  Grouping of customers by characteristics (income, location, demographics).

High-Value Customer:
  Customer with YearlyIncome > $100,000.

Geography:
  Customer location including country, state/province, and city.
  Stored in DimGeography dimension.

Household:
  Customer's family unit. Attributes include MaritalStatus, TotalChildren.

===============================================
TERRITORY & GEOGRAPHY
===============================================

Sales Territory:
  Geographic region assigned to sales team. Different from customer location.
  Stored in DimSalesTerritory.

Territory Region:
  Specific territory name (e.g., "Northwest", "Southwest", "Canada").

Territory Group:
  High-level grouping (e.g., "North America", "Europe", "Pacific").

Customer Geography:
  Where customer is physically located. May differ from their assigned sales territory.

===============================================
TIME TERMS
===============================================

Order Date:
  Date when order was placed. Primary date for sales analysis.

Ship Date:
  Date when order was shipped/delivered.

Calendar Year:
  Standard year (January 1 - December 31).

Fiscal Year:
  Company's financial year (may differ from calendar year).

Quarter:
  Three-month period. Q1 (Jan-Mar), Q2 (Apr-Jun), Q3 (Jul-Sep), Q4 (Oct-Dec).

Semester:
  Six-month period. Semester 1 (Jan-Jun), Semester 2 (Jul-Dec).

Holiday Season:
  November-December, typically highest sales period.

===============================================
FINANCIAL & METRICS
===============================================

Profit:
  SalesAmount minus TotalProductCost. Also called "Gross Profit".

Profit Margin:
  Profit as percentage of sales. Formula: (Profit / Sales) × 100.

Average Order Value (AOV):
  Average revenue per order. Total revenue / number of orders.

Units Sold:
  Total quantity of items sold (SUM of OrderQuantity).

Order Count:
  Number of unique orders (COUNT DISTINCT of SalesOrderNumber).

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS):
  TotalProductCost - what it costs to make/buy the products sold.

Margin:
  Same as profit margin - percentage profit on sales.

===============================================
INVENTORY TERMS
===============================================

Inventory:
  Stock of products on hand, stored in FactProductInventory.

Units Balance:
  Current quantity of product in stock.

Inventory Level:
  Amount of stock available for sale.

Movement Date:
  Date of inventory transaction or snapshot.

===============================================
EMPLOYEE & ORGANIZATION
===============================================

Employee:
  Person who works for Adventure Works, stored in DimEmployee.

Sales Quota:
  Target sales amount assigned to sales employees.
  Stored in FactSalesQuota (confidential data).

Department:
  Organizational unit within company (e.g., Sales, Marketing, Production).

Title:
  Job title or role (e.g., "Sales Representative", "Manager").

===============================================
RESELLER TERMS
===============================================

Reseller:
  Business customer who purchases wholesale and resells to consumers.
  Stored in DimReseller.

Business Type:
  Type of reseller business (e.g., "Specialty Bike Shop", "Warehouse", "Value Added Reseller").

Annual Revenue:
  Reseller's business revenue (not purchases from Adventure Works).

Number of Employees:
  Size of reseller business.

===============================================
TECHNICAL TERMS
===============================================

Fact Table:
  Table containing measurable transactions or events (e.g., FactInternetSales).
  Contains foreign keys to dimensions and numeric measures.

Dimension Table:
  Table containing descriptive attributes (e.g., DimProduct, DimCustomer).
  Provides context for facts.

Primary Key:
  Unique identifier for a record (e.g., ProductKey, CustomerKey).

Foreign Key:
  Column that references a primary key in another table.
  Enables joins between tables.

Surrogate Key:
  Artificial key assigned by the system (e.g., ProductKey).
  Different from natural key (ProductAlternateKey).

Star Schema:
  Database design with fact tables at center connected to dimension tables.
  AdventureWorksDW uses star schema.

===============================================
KEY RELATIONSHIPS
===============================================

Product Hierarchy:
  DimProductCategory ← DimProductSubcategory ← DimProduct

Customer Location:
  DimCustomer → DimGeography

Sales Transactions:
  FactInternetSales → DimProduct, DimCustomer, DimDate, DimSalesTerritory

Time:
  OrderDateKey → DimDate

===============================================
COMMON ABBREVIATIONS
===============================================

AOV - Average Order Value
B2B - Business-to-Business (reseller sales)
B2C - Business-to-Consumer (internet sales)
COGS - Cost of Goods Sold
DW - Data Warehouse
MSRP - Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price
SKU - Stock Keeping Unit
YoY - Year-over-Year
QoQ - Quarter-over-Quarter

===============================================
END OF GLOSSARY
